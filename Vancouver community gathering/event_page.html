Vancouver Community Gathering

Join us for an action-packed day of fun, laughter, and friendly competition! We’ll have a mix of games, trivia, and sports—something for everyone, whether you’re here to win, to try something new, or just to have a great time.

This event is all about meeting new people, building friendships, and making memories that last. Bring your energy, bring your competitive spirit (or just your good vibes), and come ready to connect with others who love fun and teamwork. Everyone is welcome—especially if you enjoy games, excitement, and a little challenge!

Because this is a FREE event, we ask everyone to help keep it safe and enjoyable by following all guidelines and expectations. Please be respectful, inclusive, and mindful of participants from different backgrounds. A positive attitude goes a long way, and we want everyone to feel comfortable and supported from start to finish.
