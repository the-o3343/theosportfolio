Buttermilk Pancake Ingredients

  • Dry ingredients
    • 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons (25g) sugar
    • 2 teaspoons baking powder
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Wet ingredients
    • 2 cups (480ml) buttermilk
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Mix all dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt).
In another bowl, whisk buttermilk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla.
Gently combine wet and dry (batter should be a little lumpy). Cook on a lightly buttered skillet until golden brown.
